Mbe is a little project I created out of a little obsession with very abstract, highly theoretical and impractical ideas/concepts. The site takes you through scenes, each of which is used to quickly and briefly introduce the user to one of the abstract concepts I have considered. Animations are used as a guide to assist in visualizing certain areas of the concept where possible. Right now, the only scene we have is the freedom scene. However, there is more to come.
